A Little About Me...

Academics!

I enjoy a strong challenge in my coursework, so some my favorite classes are Applied Machine Learning, Numerical Analysis, and Probability Theory. This Spring Quarter I am enrolled in Principles of Computer Systems Design, Computional Models, and Personal Computers.

Hobbies!

I enjoy being outdoors, whether that's biking, walking, hiking, or playing basketball with some friends and family. Career Goals!

Over the past year, I have been considering a couple options for a career in academia as a professor or in industry and a data scientist! I enjoy teaching, being in a position of mentorship, and research. I have been tutoring for a while and inspire my students to take on the same love for learning that my teachers has instilled in me. I believe that a fair intersection of these interests would be a research and development position in industry where there is still a strong collaborative and learning environment to push the world forward with innovation.
